Picturesque town
between Zadar & Biograd.

Sveti Filip i Jakov is the centre of its municipality and the tourist heart of the destination — a place where rich history meets the Mediterranean way of life, set along the Pašman Channel with views to the islets and sunsets that feel like they were arranged for you.

Stroll the kilometre-long riva at dawn, take a coffee at the old market square, then disappear by boat for the afternoon. We've been practising this rhythm for a thousand years.

HERITAGE · Since 1059

Shaped by sea,
stone & time.

Live like a local

From a Liburnian settlement three millennia old to a Roman road, a Venetian villa and a baroque church — a place that wears its history lightly.

  1. 3000 BC Liburnian settlement — dry-stone walls & tumuli
  2. 1st c. Roman road links coast to hinterland
  3. 1059 First written mention of the village
  4. 1737 Baroque church of SS Philip & James built
  5. Today A living harbour town on the Dalmatian riva

Flavours of Dalmatia

Dishes that
remember everyone
who taught them.

Coastal Dalmatian cooking, grown on a thousand small farms behind the town and pulled from the channel each morning. Olive oil, lamb on a spit, fish baked under the peka bell, and the bread our grandmothers still bake on Sundays.

Gastronomy & local specialities
LOCAL SPECIALITIES MUST TRY
Brudet od ribe Fisherman's stew with white fish, polenta and a glass of pošip from neighbouring Pašman.
Janjetina ispod peke Slow-cooked lamb under the bell, served with potatoes that soak up everything good.
Crni rižot Black risotto with cuttlefish, finished with our own oil and a squeeze of Pašman lemon.
Pašticada Marinated beef in prošek and dried plums, the Sunday dish of the whole coast.
Around the bay

Five villages,
one quiet coastline.

From Babac islet (3 minutes by boat) to the saltpans of Sv. Petar (20 minutes by bike), the surrounding places are an unhurried itinerary in themselves.

3 MIN BY BOAT · UNINHABITED Babac izlet A pine-covered dot in the channel — swim, picnic, row back by dusk.
7 MIN BY BIKE · 1,200 INHABITANTS Turanj A fishing hamlet of stone houses where the day still ends on the water.
14 MIN BY ROAD Nautika marina Berths, charter desks and the café where every sail begins.
22 MIN BY BIKE Sv. Petar Saltpans, lavender fields and the quietest sunsets on the riviera.
FERRY · 35 MIN Pašman island Olive groves and hidden coves, a short ferry from the riva.
